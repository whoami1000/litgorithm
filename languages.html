Supported Languages

Litgorithm is intended to be used globally. Hence, the app shall be made compatible in various languages.
Currently we are accepting works in 10 different languages and more languages shall be introduced later into the later versions of the app.
In case you are interested in submitting work in a language other than those listed below, you may do so. If cleared after review, we shall try to put it into future versions.
We are currently accepting the following languages:
1) Bengali
2) English
3) French
4) German
5) Hindi
6) Italian
7) Marathi
8) Portuguese
9) Sanskrit
10) Spanish
