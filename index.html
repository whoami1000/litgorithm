About Litgorithm

Many people don't have access to education today, the main reason being the cost factor. So here is a cost-free solution- Litgorithm. No cost, no adds, no account creation required. Just study and read for free offline. Every human being has right to education and our aim is to provide knowledge to people free of cost irrespective of their personal background.
Litgorithm currently works only on Windows and Linux. Mac and mobile versions coming soon!

Writing to Litgorithm

Want to share your own work or ideas with other people. Litgorithm lets you to do that!

Application process

To write to Litgorithm, you have to apply through our website. Don't worry, the application process is simple and hastle free.

Only PDFs of the work will be accepted.
The content of the PDF should be proper text and not a PDF of scanned image.
The applicant must have a valid email id.
The work submitted should be the applicant's original creation.
Your work may be used by others if you want to write anything to Litgorithm.
Any inappropriate, vulgar or controversial topic in the work shall be rejected.
All works after application shall be reviewed by our team and shall be AI checked.
The result of the application shall be sent by email within a month.
If accepted, your work shall be put on the next version of Litgorithm when released.
If rejected, the reason of rejection shall be sent via email.
You may reapply for the same work with the required changes if you wish to.

Licensing your work

Want to license your work? You can do that for free too! You can apply for a Creative Commons License(CC-BY-NC-ND) in your application. Under this license:
1) People can use your work but have to give you the right credit.
2) People can use your work for non-commercial or non-profitable work only.
3) People can't make derivatives or adaptations to your original work.

Please Note: If you wish to license your work, then your identity CANNOT be kept Anonymous on the app. In such cases your name shall be included on the app along with your work. If you wish to remain Anonymous, you can't apply for a Creative Commons License through us. In such case, your identity shall be kept confidential.

Learn more about Creative Commons License on https://creativecommons.org/share-your-work/cclicenses/
